Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 31, 2025


Thank you for using Grok Pins! This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use this Chrome extension.


Data Collection and Use

As of the current version (0.0.1), Grok Pins does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII), such as your email address, name, or unique identifiers. The extension operates locally within your browser and does not transmit any data to external servers or third parties.


Storage Permission

The extension uses the Chrome storage API to save user pinned chats locally on your device. This data is not sent anywhere and is only used to enable the core functionality of the extension.


Future Updates

In future versions, Grok Pins may collect limited personally identifiable information, such as an email address or a generated unique identifier (UUID), to better identify users using the free or the premium version of Grok Pins. If this occurs:


- We will update this Privacy Policy to detail what data is collected, why, and how it’s protected.

- You will be notified of these changes via the Chrome Web Store or within the extension.

- Any collection will comply with applicable privacy laws and be optional where possible.


Third-Party Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties, as no data is collected in the current version.


Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the extension evolves. The latest version will always be available at this URL. Please check back periodically for updates.
